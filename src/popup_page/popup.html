  • Indeed.com

Job Search Frequency

* You can always perform a search by yourself and the frequency will get pushed back depending on your selection

Combine search results?

Yes

* If "Yes" is selected and you change your search parameters, all previous job listings will be combined when the new search is performed.

Limit listings per source

* Limits the amount of top listings retrieved from each supported website.

** "No Limit" defaults to 50.

* Some user may see a window flash when performing a search or when search starts automatically from the frequency selected. This is normal behavior as a window is opened and sent to the background.

This is an open source project. No information is collected, no cookies are used. All of your preferences and the listings you bookmarked are saved inside the browser only.

If you have any suggestions for more supported websites or more functionality, do not hesitate to write.

Community contributions to the project are very welcomed.
